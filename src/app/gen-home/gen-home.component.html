About Generation Connection

The story behind this website is that of wanting to preserve the collective wisdom of each generation. I was watching a documentary about the World War 2 generation that is slowing passing away and I thought wow, so is their collective wisdom! So I started researching and found that there were literally no sites that allow us to pass on specific wisdom that is cataloged in one place for each generation. Everyone has a story and as I walk through life and meet so many different people of every background, I'm inspired and in awe of how wonderful we all are. So as I participate in this thing called life I get to be an admirer of all of you in your own unique journey as we walk this road together! I hope that you see the beautiful stories in these pages and that it makes you want to be a better person which in turn makes the world a better place because of you!
