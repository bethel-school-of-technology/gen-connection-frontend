Welcome to Generation X



Generation X is the cohort between the Baby Boomer Generation and the Millennials Generation. We were born between 1965 and 1980, although some say it could be as early as 1960 and as late as 1985. However we will use the estimation of 1965 to 1980 for this illustration. Why are we called the X generation? The term was first used in an article (see link 5 for details in wiki-genx).
