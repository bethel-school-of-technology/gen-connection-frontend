Welcome to Generation X



Generation X is the cohort between the Baby Boomer Generation and the Millennials Generation. We were born between 1965 and 1980, although some say it could be as early as 1960 and as late as 1985. Why are we called the X generation? The term was first used in an article to note how this generation seemed lost and not seen. Generation X numbers vary depending on what time span you use, but its estimated between 56-62 million, while Baby Boomers and Millennials each have an estimated 72-75 million. Gen X is sometimes referred to as the "latchkey generation" as we were often left unsupervised at home after school until our parents came home from work. This generation also has an unprecented situation in which we are supporting and educating our own children while simultaneously providing care for our aging parents.
