Welcome to Generation Millennials


Generation Millennials (or generation Y as they follow gen X) is the cohort after the Generation X born between 1981-2000. Nurtured and pampered by parents who didn't want to make the mistakes of the previous generation, millennials are confident, ambitious and achievement oriented. They also have high expectations of their employers, tend to seek new challenges at work and aren't afraid to question authority. Millennials are generally curious but have a sense of their own individuality while being very collaborative as a group. They grew up during the technology boom and are technically savvy. They want to be part of something larger than themselves and are actively engaged. One of the downfalls of this generation is that they have short attention spans and therby don't tend to stick it out long enough when the going gets tough. They as a group like their grandparents (Baby Boomers) will have long lasting influence on the culture due to their sheer size.
